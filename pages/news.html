DEBUT ANNOUNCEMENT

“LU●MINA6 Debuts With ‘Light On Me’ and a Mission of Visibility”

Seoul — K-Pop Herald

K-pop’s newest global boy group, LU●MINA6, officially debuted on April 10 with their heartfelt single “Light On Me”. Known for its soft R&B-pop production and emotional vulnerability, the track already has fans around the world calling the group “the voice of the unseen.” With six members from across Asia and Europe, LU●MINA6 blends multilingual vocals, cinematic visuals, and inclusive storytelling. “We want everyone to feel represented,” says leader Jiho. The debut MV surpassed 3 million views in 24 hours.

July 21, 2020

FIRST VIRAL MOMENT

“Zero Motion” by LU●MINA6 Goes Viral on TikTok for Its Choreo and Attitude

Bangkok — Global Pop Feed

The second single from LU●MINA6, “Zero Motion”, is proving the group is more than just concept and vocals — they’ve got moves. The sharp, fast-paced electronic track features a choreo moment dubbed “the gravity glitch,” which has sparked over 250K TikTok recreations. Leo, one of the group's main dancers, says the move was inspired by “breaking out of silence and into rebellion.” The single has charted in 8 countries and hit #1 on Spotify’s “New K-Pop Radar” playlist.
