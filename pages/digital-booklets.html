“Velvet Crown” Sets a Genderfluid Mood for Valentine’s Day

February 14, 2021 – VALENTINE’S SPECIAL SINGLE

Tokyo — Idol Culture Review
LU●MINA6’s surprise Valentine’s release “Velvet Crown” blends cinematic strings and slow-burning pop into a visual statement of queer power. The music video features all six members in fluid fashion silhouettes, challenging gender norms with elegance and confidence. “We wanted to show love that doesn’t fit into one shape,” says Xiao. LU●MINA6 Announces First World Tour ‘LUNAVERSE’ for 2026

May 8, 2025 – GLOBAL ANNOUNCEMENT

Seoul — K-Wave News
After years of building a dedicated global fanbase, LU●MINA6 has officially announced their long-awaited first world tour, titled “LUNAVERSE”, set to begin in early 2026.
